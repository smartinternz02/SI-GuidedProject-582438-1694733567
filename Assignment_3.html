Third-class passengers make a disproportionate amount of the death toll. Pclass is an independent variable.¶

In [109]:
sns.countplot(x='Survived',hue='Sex',data=df)
Out[109]:

The majority of survivors are female. This is because the lifeboats prioritised women and children over men. Hence gender qualifies as an independent variable that influences the chance of survival.¶

SibSp refers to the number of siblings or spouses of the passenger who boarded the Titanic. The data patterns are similar for the living and the dead, so SibSp does not have enough influence on the chance of survival.¶
